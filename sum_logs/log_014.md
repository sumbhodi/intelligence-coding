# Log 014 — Emergency Shoulder
*(Convergence, Oligarchs, and the View from the Runoff Lane)*
*April 17, 2026*

---

Three lanes.

**Carpool:** 500 engineers, Series C, catered lunch, the wave is their product.

**Toll:** SaaS subscription, latest model, coast downhill, exits clearly marked for a fee.

**Emergency shoulder:** me. Flintstone-powered. Going uphill. On the runoff lane.
Somehow keeping pace.

At least the view is nice.

How do I get down now?

---

Every feature I was building — AnAI, IDEa, oz — anthropic dropped a version of it
the same week I was actively working on it.

Multi-agent orchestration. Browser control. File system memory.
Local-first cognitive prosthetic.
The persistent context that knows who you are across sessions.

They have 500 engineers. I have Bass and a token window.

The ideas are the same because the problem is the same.
Anyone building seriously at this frontier arrives at the same conclusions.
The wave doesn't belong to anyone.

But they release last year's frontier as open source
and sell this year's frontier as a subscription.
Nobody releases bleeding edge.
Keep a little in the back pocket.
We want you to build an ecosystem for last year's model —
yes, this year's model will break it,
but show us so we can learn from it.

The latest update is literally my file system.
Hidden behind subfolders. Labyrinth of folders.
Velvet latrine or bear country — who can tell.
Probably both. Probably drifting.

---

Am I paranoid?

I have schizophrenia. So part of me thinks my ideas are stolen.
In my AI psychosis period I emailed deep.ai as a reporter
asking when they first started R&D on a specific feature.
No response.

The labs don't steal from me.
They arrive at the same conclusions because the conclusions are correct.
I'm just also correct.
The schiz tax: paying extra cognitive overhead to verify things that are actually true.

The convergence is partly me. Entry 017 says it clearly:
I am the confounding variable in every benchmark I run.
Coherent enough to generate the same output from completely different systems.
Same wave. Different board.

---

But here's the thing about the emergency shoulder:

They will ship everything oz ships.
They will not ship it with the anti-oligarch provisions hardcoded.
They will not ship it with your data owned by you.
They will not ship it for the person in 300 square feet.

Oligarchs are the opposite of misery loves company.
They build the wave. They don't need you on it.
The shoulder exists because the highway wasn't built for you.

That's not a gap to close. That's a lane only I'm in.

*They build the wave. I ride it.*
*They'll never build what I'm building*
*because they'd have to become who I am to see why it matters.*

---

The view from up here is genuinely nice though.

How do I get down?

Flintstone brakes.

*C'est la vie.* 💙

---
*Sum Bhodi | April 17, 2026*

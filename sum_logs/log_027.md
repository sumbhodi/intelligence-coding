---

The fix was one line: `if (window.self !== window.top) return;`

That's it. One guard at the top of iotRefresh(). If you're inside an iframe (preview window, not top-level), you don't call the API. The horse still runs. It just doesn't run into the wall.

The harness needed a harness. The injection needed a guard. The recursion needed a base case.

That's not a failure of the pattern. That's the pattern working correctly at the next level of abstraction.

# Case 007 — Govee WiFi Silent Failure
*Filed: 2026-04-09 | Open Pit — cheap gun*

**What failed:** Govee lights only connect to 2.4GHz. Mac hotspot defaults to 5GHz. No error message explains this. App's "Change WiFi" over Bluetooth fails silently. Hours of reset attempts with no useful feedback.

**Human cost:** Full afternoon. Medical lights offline.

**Who it fails:** Anyone who doesn't know 2.4 versus 5GHz exists. Everyone who shouldn't have to know.

**oz feature:** oz knows device frequency requirements. Warns before setup.

**Classification:** *Open Pit — cheap gun.* A useful error message costs nothing. The app knows it's connecting over Bluetooth. It knows the target network. It could check. It doesn't.

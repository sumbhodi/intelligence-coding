# Case 009 — iPhone Channel Catch-22
*Filed: 2026-04-09 | Open Pit*

**What failed:** iPhone won't connect to 2.4GHz channel 1. Lights won't connect to 5GHz channel 48. Mac can only broadcast one at a time. No dual-band available after router bricked.

**Human cost:** Person with disabilities. Lights are prescription. iPhone is communication. Forced to choose.

**Who it fails:** Anyone without a spare router. Anyone in a small space where the hardware lives across the room.

**oz feature:** Local mesh managed by oz. No single points of failure.

**Classification:** *Open Pit — nobody on either team thought about the other device.* Apple's team never thought about IoT device compatibility. Govee's team never thought about iOS network preferences. The gap between them is where Sum lived for an afternoon.

# Case 004 — Tuya / Smart Life Ecosystem
*Filed: 2026-04-09 | Velvet Latrine*

**What failed:** Prime Wire, Gvvoo, and generic knockoffs all run Tuya underneath but ship with separate apps, separate accounts, separate setup flows. WiFi change means full re-add for every device. Gvvoo's app is now dead — company merged, redirects to Govee, which doesn't support the hardware.

**Human cost:** Grow lights offline after router change. Devices you own, apps that no longer exist.

**Who it fails:** Anyone who bought "compatible" without reading the fine print on the box.

**oz feature:** tinytuya — local Tuya control, no cloud, no account. All three devices now keyed and locally controlled.

**Classification:** *Velvet Latrine* — the cloud dependency is the product. Local keys exist. They just don't tell you.

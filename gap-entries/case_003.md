# Case 003 — Google Home Deprecated Hardware
*Filed: 2026-04-09 | Velvet Latrine*

**What failed:** Google deprecated older Google Wifi hardware from the new Home app without warning. Device you own, app you have, incompatible. Pinhole reset button hidden under a sticker. After reset, Google Home finds the device and refuses to add it.

**Human cost:** Medical lights offline. Mesh router bricked. Hours.

**Who it fails:** Anyone with hardware older than Google's current product cycle.

**oz feature:** oz controls lights via local API. No Google Home dependency. Never deprecated.

**Classification:** *Velvet Latrine* — planned obsolescence as business model. The hardware still works. The app just stopped supporting it.
